Products

PathScope

PathScope is a software tool for pathology researchers to interact with digital slides. I am currently designing and developing this application to optimize pathology workflows to better diagnose diseases.

Collaborating with University of Maryland Medical Center pathology researchers to elicit user stories and recognize associated capability gaps.

Prototyping proof-of-concept Python GUI to validate product features against established operational objectives.

PieTime

PieTime is a web application for optimizing topping selection for group pizza orders.

Work

Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory

Technical Project Manager • May 2009 - Present

JHU/APL is the nation's largest not-for-profit University-affiliated Research Center. I was brought on board to develop and evaluate systems for mass transit and anti-submarine national security needs.

Proposed and implemented a quantitative decision-making process to determine technology suitability based on requirements established through customer and stakeholder interviews for a Congressional feasibility study impacting 350 exit lanes at 146 airports.

Transformed ambiguous objectives into traceable requirements, designed a structured test approach, mapped out project milestones, executed test events, and verified system performance for DHS TSA mass transit security

Led operator training and system deployment for large-scale security screening of 28,000 Super Bowl attendees

Implemented and integrated data-driven features deployable to 109 active Naval ships to improve track classification performance, optimize sonar display consoles, and ultimately decrease warfighter workloads

Applied Visual Solutions

Systems Engineer • May 2006 - May 2008

AVS is a small business specializing in power engineering expert systems to enhance utility asset management and power plant operational performance. I was hired to implement data trending capabilities for historian databases.

Developed user-friendly data visualization tools to calculate critical data trends and optimize power plant operations with annual savings of up to $1.2M.

Decreased software deployment times by more than 40% by automating GUI element creation, placement, and callbacks
